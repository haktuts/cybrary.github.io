What is Key Logger ???


•A key logger is a hardware device or a software program that records the real time activity of a computer user including the keyboard keys they press.


•Keyloggers are used in IT organizations to troubleshoot technical problems with computers and business networks.


•Keyloggers can also be used by a family (or business) to monitor the network usage of people without their direct knowledge.


• Finally, malicious individuals may use key loggers on public computers to steal passwords or credit card information.


•It can be used by parents to keep eye on their children's or company owner to spy on their employs.
