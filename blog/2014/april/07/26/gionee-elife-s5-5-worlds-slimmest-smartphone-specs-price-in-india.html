Gionee Smartphone

Gionee launched the world’s slimmest smartphone in India – the Gionee Elife S5.5 for a suprising price of Rs. 22,999 . The phone first announced in Mobile World Congress ( MWC ) 2014 in Februar .

After a nearly a month taken , Gionee Elife S5.5 has finally made into the Indian market and is to be available for purchase from April 2nd week, 2014 .

The phone sports a 5 inch Full HD display with corning gorilla glass 3 for scratch resistant , and also have anti-glare as well as gives good viewing angle because of its super AMOLED display technology .

Elife S5.5 got the name for its thickness – yes, it is just 5.5 mm thick and weighs 130 gms and so we call it ” World’s Slimmest Smarpthone “ beating Huawei Ascend P6 which is 6.1 mm thick .

There is a 1.7 GHz octo-core processor made from MediaTek which also has 450 MP4 GPU , which is intended to give lag-free gaming experience and multi-function capability .

Talking about Operating system , unfortunately it doesn’t have Android 4.4 kitkat which is the latest as of now, but the Elife S5.5 sports the previous version 4.2 Jellybean , and there is no any word about whether the device will get the latest update or not .

Next come the Physical memory which keeps the phone engage with multiple background system apps as well as user running apps without killing your patience , specify it , there is a 2GB RAM unit .

Internal storage comes with a 16 GB memory unit and the phone is powered by a 2300 mAH battery to keep running althrough the day .

The phone sports a 13 Mega-pixel camera at the rear end which is capable of recording a video at full HD ie ) 1080p resolution and the front facing camera features a 5 MP camera for video chats .

Elife S 5.5 has non-removable battery , even the slim slot is present to the right side of the device . The backpanel itself is a glass made of Corning gorilla glass 3 .

Gionee Elife S5.5 Price in India :

As expected the Gionee Elife S5.5 has been smartly priced and the same is going to be available for you at Rs. 22,999 .

There may be drop in the price a week or two week post release date which is mid-april 2014 .
