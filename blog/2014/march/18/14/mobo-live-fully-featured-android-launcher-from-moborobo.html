Mobo Live – Fully Featured Android Launcher from Moborobo

  • Date 18 Mar,2014 ( 272 days ago )
  • mobolive, android, launcher, latest
  • 0 Comments
  • 0 Likes

Android is trending today of-course its going crazy because of wide range of features it offers. Android apps are one main thing and only reason that made to out rank it’s competitors. From the millions of apps the most like is Android Launchers. Using android launchers we can customize your device the way you want. Here in this article we are introducing one best and fully featured Android launcher developed by Moborobo Team.

 

About MoboLive:

MoboLive Android launcher is developed by Moborobo Team. MoboLive launcher has many new features and user friendly. It has awesome layout, stunning wallpapers and beautiful themes, Infinite scroll between screens, customizable icons and labels for shortcuts and folders made this app something special and different from other launchers.

Some other Features:

  • Infinite scroll between screens.
  • Numerous fancy transition effects (stack, cube, shutter).
  • A wide range of handy widgets (weather, RAM booster).
  • Customizable icons and labels for Folders and also for shortcuts.
  • Hide apps from drawer
  • Quick access to the most Used apps.
  • Awesome feature is to lock your desktop to prevent the unexpected changes
  • You can Backup and restore your apps and other personal data time to time.
  • Counts the Unread messages and Missed Calls.
  • One more awesome feature is you can import icons from other launchers

Battery Saver:

MoboLive helps you to optimize your battery level. It will observe the battery consumption and optimize app memory so that it can help in running more time.

 

File Manager:

File manger is one more best feature that is available in MoboLive. You can manage Videos, Pics and music.

Animations and Awesome Effects:

I am sure you love the animation effects in MoboLive. This animation adds some extra beauty to your home screen while sliding and sharing. You can choose your own effect by Long Press on home screen button and then just tap on your desired effect.

 

Security:

Who don’t want to have extra security? Moborobo Team understand this and added extra Security feature. This will warns you from risky apps, Start apps and controls data usage.

 

Backup:

To preserve your Personal Data and Applications, this app certainly comes to your service. So now, no risk to lose any of it, you will already have everything backed up. To access, go to App drawer, slide to Manager tab and tap on Backup.

 

Widgets:

There are many widgets available in MoboLive. Some of them include analog clock, 365 Weather, Cleaner, Power Saving & Power Control. Long-press on an empty space on the home screen. Single tap on any widget to place it directly on your desired home screen.

 

Cleaner

  1. MoboLive also has Cleaner. Using this you can clean your phone with single one tap.
  2. Clear all/selective Apps and manage device’s memory
  3. Delete temporary, log and other junk files
  4. Manage Apps, ads from appearing in Notification Bar (for rooted phone)
  5. Disable/enable/manage the Startup Apps (for rooted phone)
  6. Detect and delete installed APK files
  7. Delete and manage other Large files like Videos, zips and much more
  8. Tap on RAM Booster icon to quickly clear and reserve the device’s memory

Conclusion:

MoboLive Android launcher is one of best launcher I have ever used. It has many features and you can customize the way you want. The Good news is that it is available for FREE in play store so hurry up and get MoboLive on your device.
