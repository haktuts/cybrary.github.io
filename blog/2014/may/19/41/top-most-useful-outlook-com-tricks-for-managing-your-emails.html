Top Most Useful Outlook.com Tricks For Managing Your Emails

  • Posted by Bhargav
  • Date 19 May,2014 ( 210 days ago )
  • outlook tricks
  • 0 Comments
  • 0 Likes

Outlook.com (previously Hotmail) is one of the most widely used free Email service by Microsoft. Like many web services, Outlook.com comes with many advance features that makes your life easier. You can create folders, create new rules to manage and sort emails, import email accounts, add aliases (add secondary email address), import email (Gmail) accounts and many more.

I have created a list of top 3 most useful Outlook.com tricks which will help you to manage and function your Outlook.com email account easily.

Add another email account

You can send and receive email from another email account in Outlook.com. To do so, follow the steps given below:

  1. Login to your Outlook.com account.
  2. Go to Options.
  3. Under “Managing your account” option, click “Your email accounts.”
  4. Here you can see two different options to manage another email account:
  5. Add a send-only account
  6. Add a send-and-receive account

Select the option you wish to opt for and add an email account to it and follow the steps on your screen to complete the set-up process. Please note that when you add a send-only email account, you are forwarding email from that specific email account.

Reply-to address

With this Outlook.com feature, you can change the default reply-to address – let people reply to an another email-address (other than the default “from” address).

  1. Login to your Outlook.com account.
  2. Go to Options and select “Reply-to address” option.
  3. Now here you can see “Your From address” is bey default selected, change it and select “Other address” and input the email address and click save.

How to use aliases to Outlook.com

An alias is an additional email address which is used in the same Outlook.com inbox and can send emails from an alias from Outlook.com inbox. It’s like using another email address from the Outlook.com inbox.

Create a Outlook alias

  1. Login to your Outlook.com account.
  2. Click your profile image (upper-right corner) and select Account settings.
  3. Under Account tab, select Aliases option.
  4. Click Add or change aliases and then click Add alias.
  5. Now you can see two different option:
  6. Create a new email address and add it as an alias
  7. Add an existing email address as a Microsoft account alias
  8. Select the one you wish to use and input the new email address
  9. Click Add alias.

Rules for creating Outlook aliases

  • You can create up to ten aliases.
  • You cannot add an existing @hotmail.com, @live.com, and @msn.com email addresses as aliases.
  • You can create an alias using Gmail, Yahoo!, or AIM Mail address.

You can also change which alias is your primary email account. To do so:

  1. Login to your Outlook.com account.
  2. Click your profile image (upper-right corner) and select Account settings.
  3. Under Account tab, select Aliases option.
  4. Click Make primary under the alias you want as your primary.

Delete or remove an alias

  1. Login to your Outlook.com account.
  2. Click your profile image (upper-right corner) and select Account settings.
  3. Under Account tab, select Aliases option.
  4. Click Remove under the alias.
  5. It’s done.
